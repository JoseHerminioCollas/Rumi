## Legal Disclaimer: RWA Compliance & Mineral Commercialization

This platform, **Rumi**, operates under the legal framework of the Republic of Peru regarding the trade and export of mineral products. By interacting with Rumi NFTs, users acknowledge the following:

### 1. Right of Free Commercialization
In accordance with **Article 4** of the [Ley General de Minería (DS Nº 014-92-EM)](https://www.gob.pe), the internal and external trade of minerals is free (**Libre Comercialización**). No special mining concession is required to buy or sell finished stone products, provided they were acquired through legal channels.

### 2. Legal Possession (Non-Concession Holders)
Per **Article 6** and **Article 263** of the same Law, the possession of minerals by retailers, artisans, and collectors is protected. For assets categorized as **Legacy/Retail**, Rumi establishes a "Point of Discovery" provenance based on the retail acquisition date and vendor data, which serves as the baseline for legal possession.

### 3. Cultural Heritage Restriction
Rumi does not tokenize or facilitate the export of items classified as **Patrimonio Cultural de la Nación** (National Cultural Heritage). All stones, particularly those with artisanal carvings or historic mounts, must comply with [Ministerio de Cultura](https://www.gob.pe) regulations. The "Validation Layer" on the Hedera network acts as a preliminary check, but does not replace official government certification for restricted items.

### 4. Export Responsibility
While Rumi metadata is designed to align with [SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe) and [VUCE](https://www.vuce.gob.pe) requirements, the physical exporter of record remains responsible for the accuracy of the **Exporta Fácil** or **DAM** filings. The NFT serves as a supporting digital "Packing List" and "Certificate of Origin" but is not a substitute for the final customs clearance issued by Peruvian authorities.

### 5. No Investment Advice
Rumi tokens represent Real World Assets (RWAs). The value of these stones depends on geological rarity, artisanal quality, and market demand. Rumi Coin and associated NFTs are not offered as regulated financial securities.
