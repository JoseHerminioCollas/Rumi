# Rumi: Terms of Service & Legal Framework

This document governs the tokenization, acquisition, and trade of Peruvian stones on the Rumi platform. By interacting with Rumi NFTs on the Hedera network, you agree to these terms.

## 1. Legal Basis for Mineral Commercialization
Rumi operates under the legal framework of the Republic of Peru. All tokenized assets are protected by the following statutes:

*   **Free Trade (Article 4):** Pursuant to the [Ley General de Minería (DS Nº 014-92-EM)](https://www.gob.pe), the internal and external trade of mineral products is free (**Libre Comercialización**).
*   **Legal Possession (Article 6 & 263):** The possession of minerals by non-concession holders (artisans, retailers, and collectors) is legally recognized. For "Track B" (Retail) stones, the retail invoice or point of discovery serves as the established legal start of provenance.

## 2. Provenance Tracks
Users acknowledge that Rumi NFTs are categorized based on available documentation:
*   **Certified Origin:** Assets backed by [REINFO](https://www.gob.pe) or [VUCE](https://www.vuce.gob.pe) documentation.
*   **Retail/Legacy Acquisition:** Assets purchased in Peruvian markets where geological origin is undocumented. These tokens celebrate the **Artisanal Value** and **Cultural Heritage** of the piece rather than its mining source.

## 3. Cultural Heritage (Patrimonio Cultural)
Rumi strictly prohibits the tokenization of items classified as **National Cultural Heritage**. 
*   In compliance with [Ministerio de Cultura](https://www.gob.pe) regulations, no stone identified as a pre-Columbian artifact or protected historical object may be minted. 
*   The **Validation Layer** (Multi-sig) acts as a preliminary check but does not supersede official government determination.

## 4. Export Compliance & Taxes
*   **Export Responsibility:** While Rumi provides digital metadata to assist with [SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe) and [Exporta Fácil](http://www.exportafacil.gob.pe) filings, the physical exporter is solely responsible for the accuracy of customs declarations.
*   **Taxation:** International exports of these stones are generally exempt from **IGV (General Sales Tax)** under Peruvian export law, provided all [SUNAT](https://www.sunat.gob.pe) protocols are met.

## 5. Metadata and Physical Pairing
*   **Digital-Physical Bond:** The NFT metadata (compliant with [HIP-412](https://hips.hedera.com)) includes optical fingerprints (inclusions) stored on [IPFS](https://ipfs.tech).
*   **Verification:** Tampering with the physical stone or its associated "Rumi ID Card" nullifies the digital provenance record.

## 6. Disclaimer of Liability
Rumi is a platform for the tokenization of **Real World Assets (RWAs)**. 
*   **Values like** market price, geological rarity, and artisanal craftsmanship are subject to fluctuation. 
*   Rumi does not provide financial or investment advice. Tokens represent ownership of a specific physical specimen and its associated cultural data.
